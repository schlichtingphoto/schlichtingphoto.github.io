Bio

I am Marc Schlichting and I am a hobby landscape photographer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I inherited my passion for photography from my father who is a hobby photographer himself and generously let me use his camera for my first photographic adventures. 

When I'm not outside and exploring the fascinating nature of California or other regions, I am a PhD candidate in aeronautics and astronautics at Stanford University.
